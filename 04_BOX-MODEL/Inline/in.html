Padding ,Border, Margin, Outline in Block and Inline Element

In inline Elemnt , Box properties works on left and right properly , while in top bottom they dont push elements instead gets over them

While In Block Elemnt , Box properties works on left , right and in top bottm they push elements -> dont get over them.

Block : They never overlap other elements , they push them .
Inline : When padding and border or other box properties gets increased , its comes over to other elements , rather than pushing them.
Block : Inline padding and border or other box properties overlap other elements , rather than pushing them
Inline : When padding gets increased or other box properties , its comes over to other elements , rather than pushing them.

Types of Inline Elements :

  1. Replaced Elements : Those who dont contain content (img ,iframe ,video tag) -> they show property of inline-block elements.
  2. Non Replaced : Those who contain content (a,b,span) -> the show property of pure inline elements.
