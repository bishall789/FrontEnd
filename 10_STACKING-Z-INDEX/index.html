STACKING AND Z - INDEX

In general STACKING works in normal way , the elements which is places at last will have highes value or it will be in top of other elements

As in given figure, red is placed first, green in second and blue in last.

We can bring any div to front of other in 2 ways :

  1. Using position other than static (by Default its static) -> So in this case red eleement has position while green and blue has not.
    2. RED
    GREEN
    BLUE
  2. Using Z-Index : The element with higher z-index will be shown on top or front. (By default Z-INDEX is auto ( 0 ))

    3. In this case , all element RED , GREEN and BLUE has position but Red has Z-index = 10 , Green has Z-index = 5 and blue has Z-index = 2.

    SO Red will be on front and then green and on back blue will be.
